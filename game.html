Max's adventure


  • Use the menu in the top left to change your appearance.
  • You can move around by using the arrow keys.
  • To go back where you started you can press HOME key.
  • To save a new starting position press the INSERT key.
  • To reset to the original starting point press the DELETE key.
  • You can also do things like:
    • J for jump
    • S for spin
    • F for flip
    • C for cartwheel
    • W for wave(hello or good by)
    • Y for yes(head nod)
    • N for no(head shake)
    • ESC for reset
